Technology And Craft

About This Lecture

With every new technological advance, the landscape of art and craft has been altered. The introduction of computers and digital technology into the artists' studio has brought up questions about the role of the hand in making, the relevance of physical objects and the merging of art and design. In this panel, Jesse Seay, Amanda Hagemann and David Demaree discussed new technologies that have effected their artistic and professional careers.
