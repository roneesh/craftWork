Drawing the Line: Art, Craft, Non-Art and Non-Craft

About This Lecture

Defining the terms "art" and "craft" is difficult enough but what about those techniques that are excluded by either term? Craft/Work welcomed a panel of artists with wide-ranging skills for a discussion about how and where we draw the line between art, craft, non-art and non-craft.

This lecture was held January 7th, 2014 at Beauty and Brawn Gallery in Logan Square, Chicago.

Panelists:

Jessica Pleyel: jessicapleyel.com
Nora Renick Rinehart: nora-renickrinehart.com
