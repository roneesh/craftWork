• About Craft Work
    Nora Renick Rinehart is both an artist and a crafter. Her practice has included making pieces to show in galleries, selling wares online and at craft-fairs and simply making for the sheer (and often nerdy) joy of it. Her current passion is teaching all sorts of textiles here in Chicago.
